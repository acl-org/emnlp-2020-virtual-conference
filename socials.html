Queer in AI

Pranav A & Vasundhara & Luca Soldaini & Sabine Weber

The Queer in AI social is an event for queer people and allies in NLP to share their experiences, network with others and discuss queer issues in research. The social will involve a mix of mini-talks, Q&A sessions and breakout discussions. All event information will remain confidential to respect the privacy and safety of participants. People of all identities, including allies, are invited to attend!

This event takes place in Zoom.

Social A: Delip, Lil Miss and Kirby: Nov 17 (01:00-03:00 UTC)
Social B: Naomi, Jasmijn and Irving: Nov 18 (16:00-18:00 UTC)

Veg*NLP

Boaz Shmueli

Vegetarians and vegans encounter fascinating challenges in many academic settings. We will discuss the multitude of issues and share anecdotes, stories and recipes as non-meat-eaters in the academic world. Everyone is welcome to join, including carnivores, omnivores, and people with religious or any other dietary restrictions!

This event takes place in Gather.Town.

Virtual Potluck Party: Nov 17 (14:00-15:00 UTC)
